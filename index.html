Message from State Attorney TaxesArentAwesome


One of the most significant duties of the Government is to provide recourse and prosecution to victims of crimes as allowed by the fullest extent of the law. We carry out this responsibility by prosecuting criminal cases, seeking accountability for offenders, and advocating for the rights and safety of our community.

Our duties include:

  1. Prosecution of Criminal Cases: We diligently pursue the prosecution of criminal offenses committed within our jurisdiction. Our experienced team of prosecutors works tirelessly to gather evidence, build strong cases, and present them in court. By holding offenders accountable, we strive to maintain public safety and uphold the principles of justice.
  2. Legal Guidance and Advice: We provide legal guidance and advice to law enforcement agencies within Broadview County. Our office collaborates closely with local police departments, assisting them in interpreting and applying the law correctly. We work hand in hand to ensure that investigations are conducted lawfully and that the rights of both victims and the accused are protected.
  3. Victim Support and Advocacy: We are dedicated to supporting and advocating for the rights and needs of victims of crimes. Our office offers various services, including victim/witness assistance programs, counseling referrals, and resources to help victims navigate the legal process. We strive to ensure that victims receive the necessary support and are treated with compassion and respect.
  4. Community Outreach and Education: We believe in fostering strong community relationships and promoting awareness of legal rights and responsibilities. Through community outreach initiatives, public presentations, and educational programs, we aim to empower residents with knowledge about the legal system, crime prevention, and their role in maintaining a safe community.

Additionally, we place great emphasis on collaboration with local law enforcement agencies. We work closely with police departments, sheriff's offices, and other law enforcement agencies to facilitate efficient and effective investigation and prosecution of criminal cases. This collaboration includes sharing information, coordinating efforts, and providing legal expertise to ensure that justice is served and our community remains secure.

I look forward to working as your State Attorney in ensuring that Broadview County remains a tourist attraction center with a crime-free environment and a welcoming atmosphere.

Yours in public service,

Taxes A. Awesome
State Attorney for Broadview County
