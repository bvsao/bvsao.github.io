Special Counsel Appointed to Investigate Criminal Activities


Delfino, FL — Acting State Attorney Vexadine announced the appointment of State Attorney TaxesArentAwesome to serve as Special Counsel for the State of Florida for any ongoing investigations into criminal activities regarding aiding and abetting of petty criminal behaviors between the dates of July 21, 2023 and August 25, 2023, as well as any other criminal allegations by, among others, County Executive Jamz0Jamz.

This decision was made pursuant to Section 201.6 of the Broadview County, FL Code of Ordinances, which requires the appointment of an independent Special Counsel to act separate from the State Attorney's Office for any criminal investigation involving elected members of the government. No charging decisions have been made at this time and this order is specifically to afford an independent investigation, review, and report, as well as to empowered the Special Counsel to act independent of the County Government. With this order, they are empowered to make any decisions regarding investigations brought by their office and may bring prosecutions in the name of the State of Florida while acting independent of the State Attorney's Office.
